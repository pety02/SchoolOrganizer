Brief description:

The aim of our web application is to provide you the opportunity to organize your school or academic life.

What we offer?
  1. Calendar panel with the opportunity to add events on it.
  2. Tasks panel with the opportunity to see all your important tasks' information, adding new tasks, editing already added tasks and deleting them.
  3. Notebooks panel with the opportunity to view, create, update and delete some notebooks' sections.
  4. Profile panel with the opportunity to edit your personal data.

Enjoy with us!
